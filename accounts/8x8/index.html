Areas of growth and complexity
1
BUNDLING
Custom multi-component bundles create a manual revenue allocation problem at every deal.
8x8's enterprise pricing page invites customers to "mix and match" UC, Contact Center, CX, and CPaaS products into custom plans. Each unique bundle is a distinct arrangement of performance obligations, meaning finance must allocate transaction price across components on every contract — a process that becomes unmanageable without automation as deal volume grows.
2
SSP ALLOCATION
Bundling software, platform, and professional services in one contract demands defensible SSP analysis at scale.
8x8 offers distinct software, platform, and Professional Services components that can be contracted together. ASC 606 requires each performance obligation to carry a standalone selling price, and when those prices aren't observable from standalone sales, finance must estimate and document SSP methodologies — a significant audit exposure if done in spreadsheets across thousands of enterprise contracts.
3
USAGE & CONSUMPTION
High-volume API and messaging usage generates continuous revenue events that can't wait for month-end reconciliation.
8x8's Communications APIs — spanning voice, SMS, High-Volume SMS, and High-Volume Messaging — generate billable usage events continuously across enterprise customers. Recognizing revenue in line with actual consumption, rather than invoicing cycles, requires a system that processes usage data in real time and maps it to ASC 606-compliant schedules without manual intervention each period.
