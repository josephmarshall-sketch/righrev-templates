Areas of growth and complexity

SSP ALLOCATION
Bundled enterprise plans create SSP allocation risk across every new contract.
Axon's enterprise agreements combine hardware devices, cloud software, professional services, and SOW-based deliverables into a single arrangement. Each element requires a standalone selling price, and with new AI products like Axon Vision and Axon 911 now added to the bundle mix, the allocation matrix grows more complex with every deal signed.
2
CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
Written change orders on active enterprise deals force constant revenue reallocation mid-stream.
Axon's enterprise terms explicitly require written change orders when scope, fees, or schedules shift — and at 34% year-over-year revenue growth, the volume of in-flight contracts being amended is accelerating. Each modification triggers an ASC 606 analysis: prospective adjustment, catch-up, or full contract re-assessment, all of which create manual work at scale.
3
BUNDLING
Named plans like the AI Era Plan and Officer Safety Plan package distinct performance obligations that must be unbundled for compliant recognition.
Axon goes to market with tailored, all-inclusive plans that mix cameras, software licenses, cloud storage, Axon Assistant AI access, and support under one price. Finance must identify and separate each distinct performance obligation inside these plans, assign SSP, and recognize each component on its own schedule — a process that doesn't scale in spreadsheets as the product catalog expands.
