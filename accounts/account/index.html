Areas of growth and complexity
1
SSP ALLOCATION
Bundled enterprise plans with hardware and services create compounding SSP allocation work at scale.
Axon's AI Era Plan and Officer Safety Plan bundle cameras, software, cloud services, and professional services SOWs into a single customer agreement. Every new enterprise deal requires standalone selling price allocation across multiple distinct performance obligations — a process that breaks quickly when volume grows at 34% per year.
2
CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
Written change orders that adjust fees or scope trigger cumulative catch-up calculations your current system likely handles manually.
Axon's enterprise terms explicitly require written change orders when contract scope or pricing changes. Under ASC 606, each modification must be evaluated as a new contract or a modification of an existing one — with potential cumulative catch-up entries. At Axon's transaction volume, these events accumulate quickly and are expensive to process without automation.
3
AI PRODUCT
Three new AI products launched in April 2026 introduce net-new recognition patterns that need to be defined before revenue closes.
Axon Vision, Axon Assistant, and Axon 911 each represent new performance obligation types — real-time AI inference, a cross-ecosystem AI layer, and a cloud-hosted 911 platform. Each needs a defensible SSP, a recognition pattern, and a clear point-in-time or over-time determination before it can be included in enterprise bundles and recognized correctly under ASC 606.
